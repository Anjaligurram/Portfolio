About Me
Designing Is My Passion

This assignment was my first time designing a product, and I admittedly started off pretty clueless. I spent hours exploring product design resources online, scouring case study after case study, and analyzing common design patterns. After I had a decent grasp of the design process, I did some very basic user research (aka interviewing a few friends), then brainstormed solutions. To translate these ideas into digital prototypes, I experimented in Adobe Xd for the first time. I ended up becoming highly invested in this technical challenge, spending 10 hours on the mock up and prototype stage alone, learning an exorbitant amount about design tools and principles in the process. Through this challenge, I realized how passionate I am about using design to solve real problems for real people.
